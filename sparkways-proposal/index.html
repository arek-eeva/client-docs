How we work on the floor

Observational, not directed. Children set the pace. Educators do what they would normally do. The crew moves quietly through the rooms and outdoor spaces, captures what is happening, and leaves the day feeling normal for the children.

We capture

  • Real moments and natural interaction
  • Immersion in play and learning
  • Curiosity, movement, exploration
  • Natural emotion and connection
  • Diversity, inclusion and belonging
  • Educator-child relationships

We avoid

  • Posed smiling directly at camera
  • Sterile or staged environments
  • Cluttered or chaotic compositions
  • Visible personal information or identifiable data
  • Anything that compromises dignity, privacy or wellbeing
  • Stock-style childcare imagery

Child safety, privacy and consent

This is the part of the work nobody outside the sector gets right. We do, because it is the only kind of work we do.

Crew clearance and signed policies

Every crew member holds a current Working with Children Check, has signed EEVA's child safety acknowledgement and contractor child safety policies, and is briefed before stepping on site. Copies of the signed policies available on request.

Centre-led on the day

Centre staff accompany the crew at all times. Educators direct what is captured and where. If a child opts out, we stop.

Model releases provided

We provide plain-English photography consent forms, ready for your team to distribute to families ahead of the shoot. Sparkways manages the family communication; we make sure the forms are fit for purpose.

Privacy-aware capture

No names, family details, sign-in sheets, learning journals or other identifying data captured in frame. We brief educators on what to clear from view.

No-consent protocols

If consent has not been obtained for a child, we do not photograph them in identifiable ways. Editorial and culling double-check this before delivery.

Footage handling

Files held on encrypted storage. Strict retention policy. No personal copies retained by contractors after delivery.

EEVA's promise: we put children's safety, privacy and dignity first. The library you receive is one we can both stand behind, today and in five years.
